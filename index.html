Abstract

MB-X.01 is reformulated here as an internal module of Tecnologia delle Espressioni (TE), not as a derivative system but as its native extension. The central function measures the semantic and logical stability of an expression through multi-base coherence, providing an operational index of functional truth.

Coherence Function

CoherenceTE(x) = f(TruthΩ, Co+, Score+)
  • TruthΩ: stability of the expression under base transformations;
  • Co+: normalized exponential measure of coherence;
  • Score+: integrates bias (B), interest (I), and confidence (C) to provide a weighted measure of functional truth.

TE Co-Observation Arc

The Dual-Echo Framework of MB-X.01 becomes the TE Co-Observation Arc: two mirrored networks observe each other to maintain representational identity continuity. Role in the TE System

MB-X.01 functions as an internal validation unit and a semantic co-resonance measure between content (coherence) and form (decoherence). It enables TE to quantify the degree of stability and alignment of a representation with respect to its semantic field.

Summary

MB-X.01 is not a derivative but a coherent extension of TE: an operational module translating the theory of interferential consciousness into a TE-native architecture, fully compatible with dual logic and the functional measure of truth.
