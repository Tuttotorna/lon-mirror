## 1. Definition (Domain-Agnostic)

### Structural Resistance (Ω)
Structural Resistance is the property of an entity to **remain non-derivable, non-collapsible, and identity-preserving** under a defined set of **information-preserving transformations**.

- Ω is **relational**, not intrinsic.
- Ω is **ordinal**, not absolute.
- Ω does **not** require numbers, units, bases, or semantics.

Formally:
> An entity has higher Ω if it resists reduction more strongly than others under the same transformations.
