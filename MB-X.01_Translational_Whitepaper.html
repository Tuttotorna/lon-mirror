4. Translational Mission

The MB-X.01 project’s purpose is not to simulate thought but to measure its coherence. The translational intent is to provide a shared mathematical and semantic ground where human introspection and artificial logic can validate one another.

In operational terms, this means:

  • Providing open metrics (TruthΩ, Co⁺, Score⁺) for evaluating model coherence.
  • Embedding append-only temporal signatures (Lya) to ensure traceability of reasoning chains.
  • Offering a neutral verification layer (Third Observer) to audit bias and hallucination sources.
